Timings

Half day standard surfing lessons are 2½ hours long which includes 2 hours of tuition. Please arrive 15 minutes before your surfing lesson starts to give yourself plenty of time to get in to your wetsuit.

Morning lessons run from 10.30 – 12.30 with check in at 10.15.
Afternoon lessons run from 14.30 – 16.30 with check in at 14.15.

Equipment

  • Full wetsuit
  • Beginner Foam Surfboard
  • Expert tuition (beach safety, step by step in and out of the water)
  • Free West Point Surf School T-shirt
